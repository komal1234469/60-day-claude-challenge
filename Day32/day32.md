* Responding to unexpected marketing events strategically.
* Reviewing campaign performance through a Growth Report.
* Using reusable AI prompts to improve marketing planning.

## Key Takeaways

* Marketing starts with understanding the audience, not creating content.
* Platform selection should match where the audience spends time.
* Strong content pillars create consistency.
* Planning ahead while staying flexible leads to better results.
* AI can significantly speed up strategy creation while leaving room for human creativity and decision-making.
