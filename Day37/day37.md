# Day 37 - Task Compass

## Learn How Work Flows Through Real Organizations

Task Compass is an interactive educational simulation designed to help users understand how tasks move through organizations and how teams collaborate to solve problems.

## Objective

The goal of this project is not to test knowledge of job titles but to teach:

* Ownership
* Delegation
* Collaboration
* Workflow Thinking
* Escalation Paths

The experience feels more like a strategy game and management simulation than a traditional quiz.

## Features

### Stage 1: Who Owns This?

Users analyze realistic workplace situations and identify the primary owner responsible for handling the issue.

### Stage 2: Task Routing

Users build the workflow by arranging teams and roles in the order tasks typically move through an organization.

### Stage 3: Collaboration Challenge

Users solve larger organizational problems by assigning multiple teams and understanding communication flow.

### Organizational Thinking Dashboard

At the end of the experience, users receive feedback across four dimensions:

* Ownership
* Delegation
* Collaboration
* Workflow Thinking

## Key Learnings

* Ownership does not mean working alone.
* Delegation is a critical organizational skill.
* Complex problems usually require collaboration between multiple teams.
* Efficient workflows reduce delays and communication gaps.
